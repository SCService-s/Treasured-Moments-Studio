Our Story

Quality, Purpose,
and Personal Touch

Treasured Moments Studio was founded with a simple idea: photos shouldn't just live on phones. They should be turned into something people can hold, display, and return to — something real.

We specialize in personalized 2"×3" photo magnets with a focus on memorial keepsakes for funeral homes and families. Every magnet is made with care and attention to detail — because the moments they represent deserve nothing less.

We also offer custom magnets for everyday occasions — pets, families, graduations, weddings, and businesses — along with photography sessions designed to produce images that translate beautifully into keepsakes.

We are local, reliable, and genuinely invested in the quality of every single order we fulfill.
